# Blacksmith Runner Types & Pricing

Source: https://docs.blacksmith.sh/blacksmith-runners/overview

## Ubuntu x64 Runners

| Instance Type | vCPU | Memory | Storage | `runs-on` Label |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2-core | 2 | 8 GB | 80 GB | `blacksmith-2vcpu-ubuntu-2404` |
| 4-core | 4 | 16 GB | 80 GB | `blacksmith-4vcpu-ubuntu-2404` |
| 8-core | 8 | 32 GB | 160 GB | `blacksmith-8vcpu-ubuntu-2404` |
| 16-core | 16 | 64 GB | 750 GB | `blacksmith-16vcpu-ubuntu-2404` |
| 32-core | 32 | 128 GB | 1.5 TB | `blacksmith-32vcpu-ubuntu-2404` |

Ubuntu 22.04 variants are also available with identical specs using the `-2204` suffix.
